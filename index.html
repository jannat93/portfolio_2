Dr. Evelyn Reed
Scientist & Researcher

Welcome! I am a dedicated researcher focused on understanding and mitigating the impacts of climate change on marine ecosystems. Through a combination of field research, data analysis, and modeling, I strive to produce insights that inform conservation policy and practice.

My Research Journey

My passion for the natural world began during my undergraduate studies and culminated in a Ph.D. where I investigated coral reef resilience. Today, as a post-doctoral fellow, I continue to explore complex environmental questions. I believe in collaborative, open science and am always keen to connect with fellow researchers, policymakers, and conservationists to work towards a sustainable future.

Core Research Areas

Climate Change Modeling

Developing and applying predictive models to forecast ecosystem responses to changing climate variables and oceanic conditions.

Marine Biodiversity

Conducting field surveys and genetic analyses to assess biodiversity patterns and the functional roles of key marine species.

Conservation Policy

Translating complex scientific findings into actionable policy recommendations for governmental and non-profit organizations.
